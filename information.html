Explanation of the the Workout Plan

This plan is for complete beginners who are about to start working out with the intention to put on weight and muslce. The repetition is mostly 8 and 10 to get the form right for the people starting to workout. To get the best result from this plan, you need to be very consistent with the plan. In other words, you have workout 3 days a week every week to see results. In addition to that, lower repetitions and increase weight after every 4 weeks. Lower the repetitions by 8 for every workout, so for example if you were doing 10 repetitions, lower to 8 and so forth. For every workout, do not go below 5 repetitions. This is a 16 weeks workout program with the meal plan to get you to gain weight and muscles. For more information or questions, contact us and seek our guidance to recieve the best results.
